We observed that programming languages kind of suck for people who are told throughout their education that they are not computationally-minded (or "not the engineering, math/science type").

Programming languages are really not good for synthetic, bottom-up, creative thinking, so we were dreaming about a language that could potentially fill that gap and equalize the playing field for everyone, regardless of their age, experience, and field of practice.

How wonderful would it be if a programming language could embody the human mind and generate methodical and creative processes simultaneously?
